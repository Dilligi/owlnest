Limited Choice of Local Restaurants

‘01

Buying or renting commercial property for the restaurant use in this area is very limited as well as obtaining necessary permits requires cumbersome paperwork and a lengthy process, which has all already been solved for Owl’s Nest.

UNIQUE LOCATION

‘02

Owl’s Nest is surrounded by nature and located next to the golf club which provides breathtaking views with gorgeous sunsets as well as fresh air that could be felt throughout the inside and outside as the short driveway submerses you to the magic of Owl’s Nest and away from busy traffic.

WINE ROOM

‘03

Wine is a separate profitable business. We will have a wine room at the restaurant with a professional sommelier, who will be empowered to independently decide on which wine to order, will host tastings and sell bottles. We provide sommeliers with great conditions and a high commision (50% of the profit, the rest goes to the restaurant) in order to attract the best sommeliers in the USA, who will believe in the restaurant potential, and will be confident they can bring good sales here and make good money.

EMPLOYEE CARE & MOTIVATION

‘04

Employees receive both salary and a percentage of the restaurant's profits, which incentivizes them to work towards its success. This way, any costs associated with broken utensils and spilled food are lowered. To reduce staff turnover, we prioritize comfort and ergonomics in the workplace, with a powerful ventilation and air conditioning system for a safe and convenient work environment. The kitchen is designed to minimize the number of steps employees need to take during a shift by 20%, helping them to stay efficient and energized. Everything from the refrigerator placement to equipment and food storage is strategically mapped out to make their job easier.

EFFICIENCY IN EVERY SQUARE METER

‘05

We got a large kitchen space, which allows us to adjust the work flows in a way that employees can move around efficiently and without hindering each other, and dirty dishes do not cross paths with the clean ones. The zoning of the dining area has also been thought out to ensure the maximum seating capacity: tables can be grouped, rearranged to fit groups of different sizes - so that the guests are always comfortable and all the arrangements are invisible and undisturbing for the others. This way we can maximize the guests accommodation and the cashflow they bring in.

QUALITY

‘06

The taste is not only based on unique and delicious recipes and their execution, but also on the quality of the ingredients used. A powerful water filter will be installed in the kitchen, and products will be purchased directly from the local farmers.

Corporate Sales

‘07

The spacious green area expends the possibilities of the property: the restaurant can host outdoor events for local sport teams, schools and corporate gatherings. Temporary tents can be added for those events to extend income further
